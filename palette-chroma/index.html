Fraction = how much of the max in-gamut chroma each stop uses (higher = punchier, more garish). Cap = hard ceiling regardless of gamut (keeps the most saturated hues from going neon).

Stops 100 and 950 aren't part of the main ramp — they're soft fill colors (hover backgrounds, selected rows), deliberately much lower chroma. Raise these if you want visible pastel tints instead of near-white/near-black.
